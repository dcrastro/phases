Moon Phases Visualization

This visualization shows the Moon's phases as seen from Earth. You can use the slider to adjust the Moon's position in its orbit around the Earth.

Check the "Geocentric View" box to switch to a view from the center of the Earth, pointing directly at the Moon.

Note the objects are not to scale and do not cast shadows.
